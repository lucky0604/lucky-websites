Privacy Policy for Billit

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Thank you for using Billit. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your data and protect your privacy.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Billit is designed as a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. All data remains on your device.

2. Data We Store

All data is stored locally on your device using secure device storage. This includes:

  • Business Profile: Business name, email, address, logo, and signature
  • Client Information: Name, email, address, and tax ID
  • Invoices and Contracts: Document content, items, and metadata
  • App Settings: Preferences and configuration
  • Purchase History: Premium subscription status (managed by RevenueCat)

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data never leaves your device except:

  • When you export documents: You choose to share PDFs via email, AirDrop, or save to Files – this is under your control
  • In-App Purchases: RevenueCat (see below) processes purchase transactions

4. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases and manage premium subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history and transaction data
  • Anonymous usage analytics for payment processing
  • Device identifiers for receipt validation

RevenueCat is a trusted payment platform. Please review their privacy policy for details:

  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted at rest on your device
  • Your device's built-in security features (Touch ID, Face ID, passcode) protect access
  • We do not transmit data over the internet

6. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete All Data: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data
  • Export Before Deletion: Export important documents as PDFs before uninstalling
  • Edit or Delete: You can edit or delete clients, invoices, and contracts within the app

7. Children's Privacy

Billit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified in the app or via email.
